National Service Scheme and National Cadet Corps

About NSS / NCC

The NSS (National Service Scheme) and NCC (National Cadet Corps) units provide opportunities for students to engage in community service, develop leadership skills, and contribute to social welfare activities.

Key Functions
  • Community service and social work
  • Leadership and personality development
  • Blood donation camps and health awareness
  • Environmental conservation activities
  • Disaster relief and emergency response
  • Rural development programs

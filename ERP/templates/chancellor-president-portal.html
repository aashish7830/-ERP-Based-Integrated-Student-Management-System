About Chancellor & President Office

The Chancellor & President Office serves as the highest executive authority of the institution, providing strategic leadership, governance oversight, and ensuring the institution's mission and vision are realized. This office coordinates with all departments and maintains the highest standards of academic and administrative excellence.

Key Functions
  • Strategic planning and institutional development
  • Governance and policy formulation
  • Academic leadership and quality assurance
  • External relations and partnerships
  • Resource allocation and budget oversight
  • Faculty and staff appointments
  • Student welfare and institutional culture
  • Compliance and accreditation management

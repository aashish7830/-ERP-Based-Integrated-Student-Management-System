About Registrar Office

The Registrar Office is responsible for maintaining student records, issuing certificates, managing examination processes, and handling all administrative documentation related to students and academic programs.

Key Functions
  • Student record management and maintenance
  • Certificate issuance (Degree, Migration, Character, etc.)
  • Examination coordination and scheduling
  • Academic transcript management
  • Document verification and attestation
  • Enrollment and registration services
